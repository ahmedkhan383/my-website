My Favorite Personality

Muhammad Allama Iqbal

Introduction

My favorite personality is Allama Muhammad Iqbal. He is one of the greatest thinkers in the history of the Muslim world and is widely known as the Poet of the East. I admire him because his ideas were not only beautiful in poetry but also powerful in shaping thoughts about identity, self-respect, and independence.

Role in Inspiring Muslims

Allama Iqbal played an important role in inspiring Muslims of the Indian subcontinent to believe in their own potential. Through his poetry in Urdu and Persian, he encouraged people to develop self-confidence (khudi) and to work hard for a better future. His famous concept of Khudi teaches that a person should recognize their own worth and continuously improve themselves.

His Poems

He is also respected for his vision of a separate homeland for Muslims, which later became Pakistan. His ideas gave hope and direction to millions of people during difficult times. Poems like Lab Pe Aati Hai Dua and Bang-e-Dra reflect his deep love for humanity, education, and spirituality.
